Know us better

At Acer Biomedicals, we are committed to revolutionizing healthcare with cutting-edge medical equipment that ensures precision, reliability, and innovation. As a leading manufacturer, we deliver a vast range of high-quality products designed to enhance patient care and streamline medical operations.

Certified with ISO 9001:13485, CDSCO, and CE Certifications, we uphold the highest standards of safety and excellence.

Our mission is to empower healthcare professionals with advanced, efficient, and trusted solutions— "Where technology meets compassion in healthcare."

Certified Healthcare Excellence

With unwavering dedication to excellence, we uphold the highest compliance standards, including ISO 9001:13485, CDSCO, and CE certifications, ensuring our products are not just reliable, and qualitative but transformative. What We Offer

Product Development

Designing and prototyping custom medical devices tailored to client needs. Research and development (R&D) for new medical technologies. Collaboration with healthcare providers to design user-friendly equipment.

Equipment Servicing

On-site and off-site maintenance of medical equipment. Scheduled servicing to ensure optimal performance. Calibration and certification of devices.

Installation Training

On-site setup and configuration of devices. Hands-on training for healthcare professionals and technicians. User manuals and video tutorials for product usage.

Quality Assurance

Functional testing to ensure product reliability. Stress and performance testing of medical equipment. Sterilization and hygiene validation services.

Supply Chain

Providing just-in-time delivery services. Warehousing and distribution for medical equipment. Managing the full lifecycle of the equipment, from production to delivery.

After Sales

Warranty management and extended service plans. Helpdesk support for troubleshooting issues. Spare parts replacement services.
